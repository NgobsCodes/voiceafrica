About me
Meet Tumi

I'm a professional voice over artist based in Johannesburg, South Africa. With a deep, versatile voice and years of experience, I've lent my voice to campaigns for some of the biggest brands in Africa and beyond — including Pringles, AFCON, CAF, ASUS, Aramex, PGA, Katiah Auto, Mall of the North, and Jacaranda FM.

I work from my own professional home studio, which means I can deliver broadcast-quality audio with fast turnaround — most projects completed within 24 hours. Whether you need a punchy commercial read, a warm corporate narration, or a calm e-learning voice, I adapt my delivery to match your brief exactly.

I pride myself on being easy to work with. Clear communication, reliable deadlines, and audio that sounds right the first time — that's what I deliver.
